Thanks for getting your kid their own Lifestory book! This page will guide you through taking the photos of your kid to make their book.

  • Find spot with light that’s not too extreme. Inside at night is too dark, direct sunlight is too bright...other than that, you’re good.
  • Try to not cut off parts of your kid. Having plenty of space around them is best.
  • Optional prompts are included to get expressions. Sometimes they help, but you know your kid best.
  • The photos don’t have to be perfect! If your kid is loose and having fun, the photos will come out better than if they’re trying to hit an exact expression. Fun is more important than precision!

Note: If you are using a smartphone, the "Choose File" buttons will open your device camera.
